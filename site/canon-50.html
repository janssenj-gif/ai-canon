The Canon 50

Pilot release pilot-v0.1. This ranks the papers domain only. Books carry no harvested metrics yet. Two signals are harvested (all-time citations and recent-citation momentum); coverage is partial and every gap is declared rather than zero-filled. A rank is not a verdict on worth. It is a transparent output of declared evidence, weights, and missing-data rules at this release date.
