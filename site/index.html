The AI Canon

A free, method-backed reference library for AI knowledge. It ranks texts, not people. It invites correction. It sells nothing.

Clause 1 — Thesis

Every other list asks you to trust the curator. This one asks you to check the math.

Curation of AI knowledge has collapsed into affiliate listicles and opinion threads, while the field itself compounds. The Canon's claim is narrow and testable: knowledge curation can be made auditable, reproducible, and challengeable. Which works belong to the canon of AI is decided by published method and verifiable evidence — citations, library holdings, syllabus adoption, sustained readership — never by taste alone, and never by anything money can buy.

The list is not the product. The method is the product. The list is its first proof.

Clause 2 — What the Canon is, and is not

A reference library with a spine.

The Canon is

  • A curated, multilingual library of the books, papers, reports, and standards that define the AI field
  • Evidence-ranked within each domain, under a published method and weights
  • Versioned: every release tagged, every change logged, every rank movement traceable
  • Open to challenge from anyone, with public resolutions
  • Free, permanently

The Canon is not

  • A ranking of people or companies — voices and organizations are described, never scored
  • A recommendation engine, a review site, or a bookstore
  • A leaderboard across domains — a standard is never ranked against a novel
  • Sponsored, affiliated, or advertised — no entry can be bought, promoted, or featured
  • Finished — it is maintained, corrected, and re-released

Clause 4 — Method statement

Rules the ranking cannot break.

The full methodology — ontology, weights, normalization, inclusion floor, and governance — is published with each release and is itself versioned. These rules are constitutional:

  1. Deterministic scoring. Identical inputs and weights produce identical ranks, bit for bit. Anyone holding the audit file can reproduce a release with one command.
  2. Provenance on every number. Each metric carries its source, retrieval date, confidence, and licence note. A number without provenance does not exist.
  3. No silent imputation. Missing evidence is recorded as missing and penalized by a published rule — never estimated, never invented.
  4. Domains never cross-rank. Books, papers, reports, and standards are scored within their own domain. ISO/IEC 42001 is not ranked against a monograph; the comparison would be meaningless.
  5. Each language ecosystem scores within itself first. Chinese works are measured by Chinese-ecosystem evidence (Douban, union catalogues, MOE textbook designations) before any cross-lingual comparison. Coverage gaps are declared, not hidden.
  6. People are context, not contestants. Persons, organizations, and platforms are curated and described. They carry no score, no rank, and never will.
  7. Manual decisions are records. Every override carries a written rationale and is published. Conflicts of interest — including works authored within Apparens — are flagged in the data itself.

A rank is not a verdict on intrinsic worth. It is a transparent output of declared evidence, weights, and missing-data rules at a specific release date.

Clause 5 — Ontology v0.2 (frozen)

The model under the library.

The ontology distinguishes canonical entities (scored by evidence) from context entities (described, never ranked) and makes governance itself part of the data: releases, challenges, and overrides are first-class, append-only records. It is frozen for the pilot; changes now require a pilot finding, not an opinion.

canonical work [book | paper | report | standard], edition context person, organization, platform, category — curated, never ranked evidence metric {source, value, retrieved_at, confidence, provenance_url} governance release, challenge, override — append-only, public edges categorized_as · authored_by · cites · has_metric · cited_in_syllabus works_at* · founded · publishes* · maintains* · challenged_by · decided_in * volatile: expires unless re-verified each release excluded influenced · promotes · critiques — opinion dressed as data; not edges

Full specification ships with the method paper and each release package.

Clause 6 — Challenge protocol

Disagreement is a feature. File it.

Anyone may challenge any entry, rank, metric, category, or method rule. A challenge is contested against the cited evidence, not against opinion — including ours.

  1. C-1
    Send the target, your claim, and your evidence to office@apparens.nl.
  2. C-2
    Acknowledgement within 7 days; each challenge receives a public identifier.
  3. C-3
    Resolution against the data: upheld challenges change the next release; rejected challenges are answered with the evidence. Either way, the record is published.
  4. C-4
    All challenges and resolutions remain visible permanently. The challenge history is part of the library.

An upheld challenge is not an embarrassment. It is the method working in public.

Clause 8 — Data & audit

The proof ships with the claim.

Every release will be downloadable as an audit package: the corpus snapshot, the weights, the code version, the per-work score breakdowns, and the single command that reproduces the ranking. If you cannot rebuild the rank from the package, the release is defective and we want the challenge.

Methodology and audit files are public. The corpus is published progressively as the library launches — each release ships its full corpus snapshot in the audit package. Original descriptions are written by the maintainer, never scraped.

The Canon uses bibliographic facts, public metadata, and explicitly licensed or lawfully accessible sources. It does not republish copyrighted texts, paywalled content, proprietary datasets, or protected expression. Where source terms restrict reuse, the Canon records derived signals only when lawful — and omits the source otherwise, declaring the gap. Cover artwork is excluded by default.

Clause 9 — Maintenance promise

Honest about cadence. Absolute about conduct.

Every update is logged. Every correction is traceable. Every ranking can be challenged. The library is maintained as capacity allows — without deadlines we would resent, and without commercial influence of any kind.

What is promised without qualification: no advertising, no affiliate links, no sponsored placement, no paid inclusion — ever. Nothing in this library is for sale, which is precisely why it can be trusted.

A canon you can check is worth more than a canon you must believe.
